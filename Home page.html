About me!

Hi, I am Sardine Dog! I am an artist and I have been since I was a
little kid. Ive always taken a liking to drawing and creating art. I have defentily come a
long way on my artist journey. I went from making stick drawings to drawing wacky looking wolves to where I am now. I am always learning and
improving my art. Even as a full time engineering student, I try my best to draw as much as I can. With the progression of my college career, I do find myself having more time
for myself. so I have taken this opputunity to become a commision artist as a part time job. I have always wanted to do
do this even when I was in middle school and my art skills where very bad. My logo is based of my recent love
of sardines in a can and my handsome dog. I love my dog more than life itself so it felt appropriate to add him to my "brand". Anyway I hope you like my art! Enjoy!
